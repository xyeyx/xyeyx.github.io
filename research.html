Here under are the list of publications and (close to) finished research projects that I have participated. Downloadable links for many of them are provided although a few are behind the paywall. Links to the replication package of some are also provided. Not all external research are shown due to confidentiality.


Global Supply Chain in General
  • Labour demand in global value chains: Is there a bias against unskilled work? 2021. With Laurie REIJNDERS and Marcel TIMMER. Earlier working paper version (2016) in circulation here.
  • Accounting for growth and productivity in global value chains. 2020. With Marcel TIMMER.
  • Italië en Spanje zijn samen economisch even belangrijk voor Nederland als het VK (Italy and Spain together are economically as important as UK for the Netherlands). 2020. With Bart LOS.
  • Productivity and Substitution Patterns in Global Value Chains. 2018. With Marcel TIMMER. Appear as a handbook chapter in The Oxford Handbook of Productivity Analysis. Library repository can be found here.

E.S.G.
  • Labour Market Inequalities: Impacts of Technology and The Changing Nature of International Trade (2000-2014). 2023. With Bart LOS and Gaaitzen de VRIES
  • Quantifying the impact of international fragmentation of production on gender inequality. 2023. With Bart LOS and Gaaitzen de VRIES
  • Functional Specialization in Global Supply Chains and the Environmental Performance of Countries. 2022. With Bart LOS.

Development and Structural Change
  • Export Diversification from an Activity Perspective: An Exploration Using Occupation Data. 2023. With Hagen KRUSE, Marcel TIMMER, and Gaaitzen de VRIES. The replication data and codes can be found in this repository. A much earlier version with a smaller sample can be found here.
  • The Impact of Technological Progress, Globalisation and Labour Mobility on Employment Structures. 2023. With Bart LOS.

Economic Geography
  • Functional upgrading and downgrading: The role of complementary value chain linkages in EU regions. 2024. With Ron BOSCHMA, Eduardo HERNÁNDEZ RODRÍGUEZ and Andrea MORRISON.
  • Regionale ontwikkeling en brede welvaart (Regional Development and Broad Social Welfare). 2024. With Hein ROELFSEMA.
  • Functional upgrading and downgrading in global value chains: Evidence from EU regions using a relatedness/complexity framework. 2023. With Ron BOSCHMA, Eduardo HERNÁNDEZ RODRÍGUEZ and Andrea MORRISON.

Services

  • Decomposing International Trade in Commercial Services, 2021. With Christopher FINDLAY and Hein ROELFSEMA.
